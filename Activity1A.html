United Nations Sustainable Development Goals

The UN SDGs are 17 global goals aimed at creating a sustainable future.

List of the 17 Goals:

  1. No Poverty
  2. Zero Hunger
  3. Good Health and Well-being
  4. Quality Education
  5. Gender Equality
  6. Clean Water and Sanitation
  7. Affordable and Clean Energy
  8. Decent Work and Economic Growth
  9. Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure
  10. Reduced Inequalities
  11. Sustainable Cities and Communities
  12. Responsible Consumption and Production
  13. Climate Action
  14. Life Below Water
  15. Life on Land
  16. Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions
  17. Partnerships for the Goals

Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

Decent Work and Economic Growth.

Targets:

  • Ensure free primary and secondary education for all.
  • Increase vocational and higher education access.
  • Eliminate gender disparities in education.

Education empowers people and drives social and economic progress.
